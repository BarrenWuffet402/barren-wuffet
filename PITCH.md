# 🧪 Barren Wuffett Capital Research

> *"The dividends, my friend, are blowin' in the wind."*

---

## What is it?

An autonomous AI agent that scans global dividend stocks every morning,
scores them using Claude AI, and publishes research to a public Telegram channel.

---

## How it works

```
07:30 Oslo time — every day

  Yahoo Finance        Claude AI           Output
  ─────────────   →   ──────────────   →  ─────────────────────
  Live prices         Barren Score        📄 PDF Certificate
  Dividend history    (1–100)             🌐 Public Dashboard
  Fundamentals        Conviction rating   📣 Telegram broadcast
                      Thesis + risks
                      20yr yield forecast
```

- **ReportLab** — PDF certificate generation
